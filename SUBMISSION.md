# MantlePulse — DoraHacks Submission

> **AI protocol-health intelligence for Mantle DeFi — every signal benchmarked on-chain.**
>
> **Track:** AI Alpha & Data (Mirana Ventures) · **Network:** Mantle · **Cost to build:** $0

---

## 1. The problem

DeFi risk is detected *after* it hurts. Liquidity drains, throughput collapses, and
protocol anomalies usually surface on Twitter hours after the on-chain damage is
done. Existing "alpha" tools mostly re-package the same whale-wallet flows everyone
already sees, and they treat Mantle as an afterthought. There is no
**Mantle-native, real-time, verifiable** early-warning layer for protocol health.

## 2. The solution

**MantlePulse** is an autonomous AI agent that:

1. **Reads live Mantle data** — network gas utilization & throughput from a public
   Mantle RPC (real, free), plus per-protocol liquidity dynamics across the Mantle
   DeFi stack (Merchant Moe, Agni Finance, USDY/mETH RWA).
2. **Detects anomalies** with unsupervised statistical models (rolling-baseline
   z-scores) and computes an explainable **health score** and **risk score** per
   protocol.
3. **Records every signal on-chain** in `SignalRegistry` on Mantle — a permanent,
   tamper-evident benchmark of the agent's decisions (on-chain AI benchmarking).
4. **Publishes** the intelligence through a clean public dashboard and a Telegram bot.

## 3. Why the AI is substantive (not a chatbot wrapper)

The AI is the product: unsupervised anomaly detection over live time-series, with
a transparent, defensible scoring function (z-score → risk/health basis points) and
human-readable narration generated by free templated logic. Outputs are **verifiable**
— each on-chain signal stores a `dataHash` of the evidence snapshot, so any third
party can audit what the agent saw and decided.

## 4. Mantle integration (core, not cosmetic)

- **Mantle as the data layer:** the agent's primary live signal is real Mantle
  network health read directly from Mantle RPC.
- **Mantle as the settlement/benchmark layer:** every agent decision is written to
  `SignalRegistry` deployed on Mantle — Mantle is where the agent's track record
  *lives*, not just where a token contract happens to sit.
- **ERC-8004 identity:** the agent registers in `AgentIdentityRegistry` and is
  issued a unique **AgentID identity NFT**, making it a first-class, attributable
  actor in the Mantle agent economy.

## 5. The two hackathon "defining features" — both implemented

- ✅ **On-chain benchmarking of AI** — every decision + outcome recorded on Mantle.
- ✅ **ERC-8004 agent identity** — the agent holds a unique identity NFT.

## 6. Why it's novel + investment-useful (for the Mirana rubric)

- **Insight value:** Mantle-native protocol-health early-warning signals that are
  difficult to obtain elsewhere — not a generic whale tracker.
- **Data-source quality:** prioritizes Mantle-native, live, on-chain data.
- **Investment utility:** a VC monitoring a Mantle portfolio gets actionable,
  attributable risk scores and anomaly alerts with on-chain provenance.
- **Scalability:** the agent is config-driven — new protocols are one entry in
  `agent/config.js`; the same pipeline scales across the whole Mantle ecosystem.
